Self Introduction

About Mukul

I am a sales and technical leader with over 34 years of experience in the water and wastewater treatment industry. My work spans India, Nepal, Oman, the Philippines, and Africa, where I have developed markets for industrial and speciality chemicals, RO and water treatment plants, and associated equipment.

I’ve successfully promoted solutions from leading global organizations such as Thermax, Ion Exchange / Grace Dearborn, Nalco, Genesys, Norit, Wellman Robey, Temak (Greece) and Wex (Aquatech, USA), consistently driving growth and improving customer satisfaction.

During my career I have held roles including Technical Head, Divisional Manager, and Country Manager, leading cross-cultural teams, managing P&L, and building strong client relationships in multiple regions.

Strategic Focus

Business Proposition for Oman

Market Opportunity

  • Oman’s water treatment chemicals and systems market in the non-project segment alone is valued at > 1,000,000 OMR and is growing with demand for RO, wastewater, and industrial solutions.
  • Increasing need for antiscalants, corrosion inhibitors, descaling chemicals, biocides, coagulants, flocculants, and disinfectants across commercial, institutional, and industrial sectors.
  • Growth drivers include industrialization, hospitals, hotels, government initiatives for sustainable water management, increased tenders, and rising water quality awareness.

Vision & Objectives

  • Vision: Build a reliable local source of water treatment chemicals and equipment in Oman.
  • Mission: Deliver cost-effective, high-quality products with local blending and assembly to reduce import dependency.
  • 5-Year Objective: Capture 10–15% market share with strong technical support and service.
  • Profitability: Targeting ~50% gross margin with in-country development and efficient operations.

Target Customers

EPC and O&M contractors, industrial clients, and institutions, including:

GALFAR, OSCO, ATKINS, STS, Al Tasnim, Airmech, Drake & Scull, Al Ansari, industrial areas such as Sur, Sohar, Nizwa, Rusayl, and Muscat, as well as municipal authorities and large developments (e.g. water townships, malls, hospitals, hotels).

Business Model

  • Establish LLC with local blending facility near Muscat or another industrial hub.
  • Import raw materials and key components; blend chemicals and assemble equipment locally.
  • Leverage strong technical service and consultancy to drive long-term contracts.
  • Focus on cost competitiveness, fast delivery, and application support as key differentiators.
